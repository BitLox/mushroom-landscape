Deflationary Mechanics

To combat inflation and reward long-term holders:

  • Transaction Tax: 1% on buys/sells (4‰ to liquidity pool, 2‰ burn, 2‰ redistributed to holders as reflections, 2‰ to PTSD wallet).
  • Auto-Burns: Every milestone (e.g., 10k holders, game beta launch) triggers a 1% supply burn.
  • Buyback & Burn: 20% of dev funds used for periodic buybacks during dips, burned immediately.

Result? Supply shrinks over time, potentially driving value as adoption grows. Simulate it: At current tax rates, after 1 year of average volume, ~5-10% of supply could be burned.
